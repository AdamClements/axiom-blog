**AXIOM SYSTEMS — FIELD BULLETIN 001**
*Distribution: All Field Engineers*
*Date: March 2051*

---

This channel is now active.

Axiom Systems Field Communications Division will be issuing periodic bulletins to all registered field engineers regarding the status of the TR-16 drone programme, hardware updates, and operational advisories.

The TR-16 processor has completed field trials. All core subsystems are operational:

- **TR-16 CPU** — 16-bit word-addressed, 8 general-purpose registers, 35-instruction set including vector pair operations
- **PI-40 CRT Terminal** — 40x15 character display with circular buffer scrolling
- **Keyboard Interface** — mailbox-protocol input with modifier support
- **Axiom Scanner** — DMA-based sweep with sorted target returns
- **Griffin UNassembler (GUN)** — ranged engagement system with IFF targeting
- **Ironhorse Motor** — single-tile pathfinding with status reporting
- **Axiom Flasher** — adjacent-tile firmware deployment with compile-on-flash

Field engineers can now write, compile, and deploy firmware to drone units through the standard CRT terminal interface. The compile-and-flash pipeline is fully operational — Ctrl+C from the terminal triggers assembly and deployment to adjacent units.

Further bulletins will follow as additional subsystems clear field trials.

*— Axiom Systems Field Communications Division*

*Report hardware faults to your sector quartermaster. Do not attempt field repairs on the TR-16 CPU module.*
